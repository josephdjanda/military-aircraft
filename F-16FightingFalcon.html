F-16 Fighting Falcon:

Overview:

The F-16 Fighting Falcon is one of the most versatile aircraft in the U.S. Air Force inventory. The F-16 Fighting Falcon has been a key aircraft of the Air Force's aerial combat fleet. It has been in service since 1979. The figther has been adapted to complete a number of missions, including air-to-air fighting, ground attack and electronic warfare. It is an extremely manueverable aircrat making it an elite figther.
