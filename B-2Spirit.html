B-2 Spirit:

Overview:

The B-2 Spirit is a stealth bomber. It was first designed during the Cold War and is capable of delivering both conventional and nuclear munitions. The B-2 can bring firepower anywhere across the globe. There is a saying that goes like, "If you can see the B-2, just know that you are not its target." It has extremely high tech stealth and can penetrate any enemey defenses.
