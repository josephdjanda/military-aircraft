B-52 Stratofortress:

Overview:

The B-52 Stratofortress is one of the Air Force's top bombers. It is a massive plane that carry a massive payload. It has the capabilities to travel 8,800 miles and it is used as along range bomber in the United States military. The B-52 is an iconic U.S. military plane and it first entered service in 1955.
