F-22 Raptor:

Overview:

The F-22 Raptor is the first 5th-generation fighter in the U.S. Air Force inventory. It is a highly capable aircraft that uses low observable technologies, modern avionics and efficient engines to provide air superiority unmatched by any other modern military. The F-22 cannot be matched by any known or projected fighter aircraft. The F-22 is a critical component of our military and is designed to project air dominance and rapidly defeat threats attempting to deny access to our nation's Air Force, Army, Navy and Marine Corps.
